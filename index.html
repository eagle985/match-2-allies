Flag Match

How To Play The Game

This is a game of concentration, memory and visual acuity using national flags. Players alternate to turn over two cards at a time. If a match is made with identical flags, then the player scores a point and the matched cards are removed from the board. If no match is made, then the cards are turned face-down again to resume play by the other player. The first player to get 5 matched pairs wins the game.
